Search syntax

Examples:
  • "china" - searches china in title
  • "china | japan" - searches china, or japan in title
  • "title = china" - searches anything with China in title
  • "tags__tag = tag" - searches links with tag
  • "bookmarked = 1" - all bookmarked link
  • "(title = china | title = japan) & bookmarked = 1" - all bookmarked link, with china, or japan in title
Available fields:
  • "title", "description", "link", "source", "bookmarked", "dead", "artist", "album", "user", "language", "thumbnail"
Available foreign fields:
  • "tags__tag", "votes__vote", "source_obj__title", "source_obj__category", "source_obj__subcategory"
Available comparison operators:
  • "==" for exact compare, "=" to find items containing string, ">" for comaparing if greater than, "<" for comaparing if less than, ">=", "<=", "~" for negation, "is null"
Available condition operators
  • "&" - And, "|" - Or, "~" - Not
System accepts brackets.
